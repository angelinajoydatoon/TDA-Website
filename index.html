About Us

Tradean Democratic Alliance is the only legitimate partylist in ISAT U accredited by the Office of the Student Affairs And Services.

Three years ago, a group of ambitious young Tradean leaders set out to establish an organization with the goal of encouraging both leadership and academic success.

The Tradean Democratic Alliance is a group of students committed to serving our peers and protecting their rights through acts of Good Faith, Justice, and Democracy.

PROJECT IGPAT: Empowering Communities Through Health and Service

Date: May 16, 2026 | Location: Barangay Sooc, Arevalo, Iloilo City

The spirit of volunteerism and youth leadership came alive as the Tradean Democratic Alliance (TDA) proudly partnered with The Outstanding Students of Iloilo Awards (TOSIA) 2026 for Project IGPAT: Integrated Guide for Personal Awareness and Transformative Health held at Barangay Sooc, Arevalo, Iloilo City on May 16, 2026.

Anchored on the advocacy of promoting health literacy, hygiene awareness, and sustainable sanitation practices, the activity gathered student leaders, volunteers, and community members in a collective effort to create a healthier and more informed community.

This partnership reflects the unwavering commitment of the youth in becoming catalysts of positive change, proving that leadership is not only about serving, but also about uplifting communities through compassion, action, and purpose.
